NULL PIECE

A project for an artwork consisting of a single form.

The form will be enclosed within a container.

The container will not be considered part of the art.

The art will be beyond physical sense.

The art will be documented by schedules and diagrams describing its precise characteristics.

These documents will provide the idea with a specific physical or conceptual focal point.

Therefore the documents will be kept secret.

The physical particulars of the art work will be nullified in favor of the conceptual particulars of the documentation. The conceptual particulars of the documentation will be nullified so that no material significance may be attatched to the work.

Mel Ramsden, Null Piece

July 1969, Art Press
